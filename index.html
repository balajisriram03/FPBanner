"NH 44 - India's longest and most scenic national highway."

NH 44 is India's longest and busiest national highway, running from Srinagar to Kanyakumari, passing through 11 states and several major cities, serving as a lifeline for transportation and offering a glimpse into India's diverse landscapes and cultural heritage.
